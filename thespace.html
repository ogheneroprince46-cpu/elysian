THE DINNING ROOM

MAIN DINING

Welcoming 70 diners beneath a stunning architectural installation, the grand dining hall offers a refined atmosphere where industrial concrete meets polished timber, and ambient light frames a remarkable culinary journey

Bespoke tables fashioned from rich, sustainably sourced timber ground the interior, as custom brass fixtures radiate a soft brilliance throughout. Each element from hand-laid tilework to seasonal floral accents is intentionally chosen to elevate your experience.

COCKTAILS & SPIRITS

The Bar

A curated collection of rare vintages including Louis XIII lines our backlit display. The intricate mosaic floor beneath your feet and the warm, amber glow from above create an atmosphere that invites you to stay a while.

Our expert bartenders shake and stir cocktails that elevate the unique Pan-African profile of our dishes, highlighting native botanical blends and expressive spirits

WINE PROGRAMME

The Wine Cellar

A curated library of heritage vineyards spanning the globe rests within our temperature-controlled cellar. The cool, quiet stone surroundings and the soft, ambient lighting create a sanctuary designed for the true oenophile.

Our curated list offers perfect accompaniments to the adventurous Pan-African menu, emphasizing unique profiles from historic regions and innovative local winemakers.
